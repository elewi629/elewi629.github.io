About Eden Lewis

Eden Lewis is a rising Junior Fashion Design major. She wishes to go on to study more about the technique and methodology behind fashion design. Owning her own successful label is Eden's biggest dream. Some elements of her style include feminine silhouettes, pastel colors, toile patterns, and flat collars.
